#### Mac使用sourcetree跳过登录

**安装sourceTree跳过登录**

------------
* 打开sourcetree
* 关闭sourcetree
* 命令终端输入defaults write com.torusknot.SourceTreeNotMAS completedWelcomeWizardVersion 3
* 打开sourcetree即可跳过登录
